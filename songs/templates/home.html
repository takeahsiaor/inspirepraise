Scripture and (more importantly) the God of scripture inspires our praise so where better to look for song selection suggestions? Search by verse to find relevant songs!

We've all been there...looking for the correct song chords and in the right key. Preview and download chords that have each been individually checked and transpose them to the key you want. There's even recommended keys for congregational singing!

Find the songs you want? Create a setlist where you can add notes to each song, determine play order, and more!


Create or join ministries where you can share setlists you've created with other members of that ministry. Gone are the days of sending out the setlist to your worship team through an email with attachments and links to chords.

Tag songs with verses that they go particularly well with so others in the future can find it. Every tag you make helps the worship community and further edifies the body!

Do you use presentation software like Propresenter or Mediashout? Download the lyrics for songs to upload directly into presentation software and save yourself the trouble of manually typing them in!


InspirePraise keeps track of songs you've used to provide you with information about how often you use that song, when you last used it, and what key you typically use. You can also compile all your song usage stats for easy CCLI reporting!
