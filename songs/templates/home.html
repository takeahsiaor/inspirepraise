Getting Started is Easy...

Step 1: Tag Songs

The InspirePraise database collects data submitted by users about which songs are used for specific scripture passages. The more times a song is tagged to a verse, the higher the song will appear when another user searches for songs based on that verse! InspirePraise is only as good as your contributions make it so get tagging!
(Learn More...)

Step 2: Search for Songs

InspirePraise allows you to search for songs based on specific verses. You are no longer limited to searching through preset theme categories that can be vague or inconsistent. The search will give a list of songs that have been previously tagged with the same verses you searched for.
(Learn More...)

Step 3: Worship

InspirePraise helps to streamline the process of song selection for your corporate musical worship time so you can spend more time worshipping through music than worshipping through song selection!
